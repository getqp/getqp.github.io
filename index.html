checkout groceries on your phone

We're bringing checkout to the 21st century.

QuickPick is revolutionizing how you buy your groceries. With just a scan, tap and verify, QuickPick makes buying groceries at your favourite retailer as easy as 1-2-3 without the hassle of waiting in long lines or waiting for your cashier to check every item.


Scan

Using just your Android or iPhone camera, you can add an item to your cart instantly, by just scanning the barcode. If you wish to remove your item, its as easy as deleting it from your cart on the app.


Pay

When you signup with QuickPick, we'll have all of your payment details, so you won't have to worry about getting your credit card out. When you are ready to pay, just press tap on your phone and you've just bought your groceries.


Verify

Depending on your retailer, you may be required to verify your purchases before leaving the store. With our retailer app and integration with existing scanners this will be less than 60 seconds, but you can expect it to take on average around 30seconds.
